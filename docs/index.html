Clan Rules

Requirements for Membership:

  • Minimum Level: NONE
  • Donate Thorium – some clans have minimum donation rules. I don't really want to do that; just donate whatever you make. If you don't win a lot of battles and don't make much Thorium, no worries, give what you can. This is going to benefit ALL of us. Regarding the Starbase, I like our system of voting on what to upgrade next; any ideas about how to improve our Starbase more quickly are welcome
  • Be respectful – This means no bullying, no racism, no insulting each other or saying negative things about any other member. If there are any problems with anyone, let me know and I'll try to mediate. If that doesn't work, I have no problem dismissing any bad seeds. I'll do my best to treat everyone as respectfully as possible and help you all as much as I can.
  • Please don't dismiss, demote or promote other members without discussing it with the rest of us. I don't want to lose members randomly and I would like promotions or demotions to have a good reason. I have given promotions for Thorium donations in the past and would like to formulate a better system than that. Sure, showing you're helping the team with Thorium may be a good reason for the first promotion, but I believe rank is something to earn. I mean, we are already donating the Thorium we make, I would like future promotions to have more meaning.

Leadership Statements

Walktimus Prime

“Ravenous Cannons is my best attempt at forming a strong community based on values that are important to me. I actually didn't even create this clan myself, though I'm grateful for the way things turned out. I only started playing early 2024, and I was maybe a few months in. I joined a Clan that was open to all and went about trying to get better at the game. Nobody really communicated in our Clan, so I was pretty much on my own figuring things out. With only a hazy understanding of how things worked, I still managed to donate Thorium as was suggested in-game. One day, I was shocked to receive the message that I was chosen by the game to become the new Clan leader. Turns out, the old leader hadn't played for 30 days and leader position goes to the player who donates the most Thorium. So that was how it all started.”

Mission Statement:
Ravenous Cannons is a War Robots Clan with a solid moral stance and strong sense of community. Our focus is on developing an enduring army of warriors by assisting each other with tactics, strategy and ideal builds and bot setups. The key tenets of our Clan are Community, Consideration, Respect, Support and Helpfulness.
